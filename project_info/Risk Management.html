Risk Factor

Decades of Expertise and Early Support

We have over 20 years of experience in the cannabis industry and decades in the payment space. Tangi appeared on investors' radars in 2021. We garnered the early support of over 2,000 token holders, which has been vital to our progress. Our earliest movers have taken on much of that risk, allowing us to create a strong foundation for future investors.

Encouraging Informed Investments

The possibility of us making a series of bad judgment calls is unlikely but not impossible. Our early members can confirm that we do not rush into decisions, making this outcome extremely unlikely. Indeed, the pace at which we operate has often been a bone of contention between the team and our earliest investors. We always prioritise the project's success ahead of anything else, and although we are confident you can remove this particular risk factor from your list, nothing can ever be dismissed.

The Impact of Federal Legalization

The legalization of cannabis beyond state law to full federal legalisation is inevitable and will happen eventually, and that could possibly be viewed as a risk factor. However, even if the federal government were to announce legalization tomorrow, it is estimated that it could take 5 to 10 years before it becomes a reality. A significant task lies ahead in creating a framework for how federal legalization will be implemented, meaning the window of opportunity will remain open for quite some time.

Minimizing Risks Through Thoughtful Decision-Making

Every investment involves a certain level of risk, and in some cases, you can lose some or all of your investment. We cannot rule out that possibility; however, we have been very careful in our decision-making to date to reach our current position of strength and will continue to do so as we move forward. We could not be better positioned to attack the market than we are today. Nevertheless, we strongly encourage you to conduct thorough due diligence.
