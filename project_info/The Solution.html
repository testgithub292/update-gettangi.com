The Solution

Our team has developed a legal payment solution for the cannabis industry which meets all regulatory standards. According to retailers facing payment problems in the cannabis industry, our team appear to have developed the first legal and compliant payment solution they have ever witnessed. Imagine not only being a payment provider, but
the only legal payment provider for an entire industry. The potential for market dominance is unprecedented in the modern financial landscape. This isn't just a first-to-market payment solution; it's a paradigm shift in how cannabis businesses can operate financially.
Compliance and Trust : Our payment solution meets all regulatory requirements, providing a transparent and trustworthy option for 8000 retailers and 60 million consumers for the first time in the industry’s history.
Market Capture : We easily onboard customers to our system by offering the familiarity of a card payment option.
Token Adoption Strategy : Once customers are in the ecosystem through their card payment transactions, we begin transitioning them to the token-based system.
Industry-Wide Impact : With the legal cannabis market valued at a staggering $33 billion in annual sales, the potential impact of this solution is enormous. Essentially, we use the familiarity and convenience of card payments as a customer acquisition tool which opens up the pathway for the token. Our technology enables us to communicate directly with consumers, first through their cell phones and later through our app. Once consumers execute their first transaction, we open up lines of communication, incentivising them to switch them from card to token payments.
The 3 parties involved in the payment transaction benefit financially when the token is used as payment.
Retailers benefit from lower transaction fees, saving them tens of thousands of dollars yearly.
Consumers earn more rewards, discounts, loyalty points, free promotions, and other related benefits when paying by token.
We, as the payment service provider have multiple motivating factors to drive token adoption. Top of the list is our margins. Our profit margins increase when consumers pay by Tangi Token. Additionally, our ambition is to be completely independent of the banking and regulatory systems. By utilizing the token as a currency, we can control the payment process from start to finish. This allows us to eliminate reliance on card networks, banking institutions, and the ever-evolving regulatory landscape surrounding payments in the cannabis industry. Lastly, when we have established our presence in the cannabis industry, we plan to leverage our token's volume and liquidity to expand into other retail sectors, adding further value for our investors. Our journey starts by capitalizing on the enormous potential of the cannabis industry. The revenue potential in the legal cannabis industry is nothing short of extraordinary. It's not just about processing payments; it's about becoming the financial backbone of a rapidly growing, multi-billion-dollar industry. Penetrating this market isn't just an opportunity – it's a potential goldmine. Here's why:
Transaction Fees : With $33 billion flowing through the industry annually, even a modest transaction fee could yield astronomical returns, catapulting our company into the bif leagues of fintech. Imagine capturing just 1% of each transaction – that's $330 million in annual revenue!
Premium Pricing : Although we plan to offer a level playing field to the industry, as the sole compliant payment provider in the industry, we could command premium fees if we wanted to. Dispensaries and cannabis businesses, desperate for a legal alternative to cash, would likely accept higher rates for the security and convenience offered.
Ancillary Services : Beyond basic transaction processing, there's potential for value-added services like inventory management, compliance tracking, and financial reporting tailored to the unique needs of the cannabis industry.
Brand Recognition : Becoming synonymous with cannabis payments would build incredible brand equity, helping us to move into other retail industries.
Innovation Driver : The unique challenges of the cannabis industry has spurred us to develop cutting-edge payment technologies with applications far beyond this single sector. As the cannabis industry continues to grow and evolve, the need for robust, compliant financial solutions becomes ever more critical. 8Tech's dual payment system isn't just filling a gap in the market; it's creating a new standard for how business is done in this burgeoning industry.
For cannabis businesses, this means: Reduced risk and increased legitimacy Improved customer experience and loyalty Access to data-driven insights for better business decisions
For consumers, it translates to: Convenience and familiarity of card payments Enhanced security and privacy options with token payments Rewards programs that incentivize loyalty
