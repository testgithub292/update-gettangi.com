Our story

Our Story

Tangi has been in development for 5 years. Our earliest supporters have been with us since late 2021. Initially, the project was intended to be a crypto-only payment solution for the legal cannabis industry, but it has evolved into something much more. At the beginning of our project, we conducted a private presale intending to publicly list the token in 2022. We made a last-minute change to our original business strategy, which, understandably, ruffled the feathers of some of our initial backers. However, most members agreed that the change significantly improved the token's prospects, even though it caused a delay in the process. Our decision has turned out to be a masterstroke and will benefit all our investors, existing and new. Our project has a high level of credibility. We have members who have consistently held us accountable. Our community is very active and vocal, and we do not doubt they will keep us on our toes in the future. We have always been under intense scrutiny, but we see it as a positive thing.

Why did we change?

Major banks and credit card networks, such as Visa and MasterCard, do not service the legal cannabis industry, forcing 8,000 retail stores and 60 million customers to operate on a cash-only basis. In 2024, consumers spent $33 billion cash in these stores. As a result, a highly lucrative market awaits a payment provider capable of offering banking and payment services that meet all regulatory requirements. However, success hinges on the provider securing the backing of a banking partner. We cannot overstate the importance of having a banking partner if one wants to succeed in this market. This challenge has proven insurmountable to all other providers and is the biggest contributing factor behind their failed attempts to enter the market as a payments service provider. We were presented with the opportunity to succeed where others had failed. We set out on a mission to create a token-based payment solution for the cannabis industry. We were on the brink of launch when an unprecedented opportunity arose – a chance to partner with a bank and develop the first-ever legal card payment solution for the cannabis industry. It was a pivotal moment. The decision to pivot wasn't easy; it meant delays and potentially disappointing early investors. But as CEO Ron Strauss puts it: "We knew we couldn't pass up this opportunity.

Revolutionizing Cannabis Payments

It was a chance to not just innovate, but to revolutionize the entire industry's financial infrastructure." What emerged from this strategic shift is nothing short of revolutionary: a dual payment system that offers both card and token payment options for the cannabis industry. What initially seemed like a detour has become a masterstroke of business strategy. The card payment solution isn't just a product; it's a customer acquisition tool that paves the way for wider adoption of Tangi’s token-based system. The core of this investment offering is our position as trailblazers who have overcome this challenge. We have secured the most crucial ingredient for success in this market, something that has evaded all other providers in the industry. This achievement allows us to be the first service provider in the industry’s history to transparently offer a legally compliant payment service and a full suite of banking facilities to 8,000 retailers. This gives us a crucial competitive advantage.

A New Standard for Investment

We do not describe Tangi as a stereotypical crypto project, nor do we describe it as a traditional investment offer. We see Tangi as a hybrid of both and worth the consideration of al investors alike. We have defied convention by dismantling the limiting and restrictive benchmarks, such as rigid roadmaps and inflexible expectations. In doing so, we have given ourselves the flexibility to deliver a significantly enhanced project with a higher probability of success. Our earliest members have afforded us the resources and patience that allowed us to get to where we are today. They can testify to the fact that we have not taken any shortcuts. This project presents a solid foundation of conventional investment values while carefully bringing together the best of what today's novel crypto world is offering. Compared to other tokens in the market, this one stands out because of its ability to solve a real-world problem for 8000 retailers. Additionally, it has 60 million potential end-users, all highly incentivized to adopt our token. We are comfortable with our decisions to date and will continue to prioritize the success of the project.
