Bridging the Banking Gap in the Cannabis Industry

We provide the first legally compliant financial solution for the $33-billion unbanked cannabis market

addressing what others couldn’t achieve

The Problem

Challenges in High-Risk Sectors

Traditional businesses easily secure banking and card payment processing, while high-risk sectors like gaming and gambling face additional obstacles, relying on third-party providers with specific banking partnerships.

Exclusion of the Legal Cannabis Industry

Unlike other industries, the legal cannabis sector is completely excluded from banking services, as major banks and card networks refuse to serve them due to federal regulations.

Impact on Dispensaries and Market Growth

With no compliant banking or payment solutions, 8,000 cannabis dispensaries in a $33-billion market are forced to operate on a cash-only basis, limiting growth and creating operational challenges.

Our Solution
We are the first to offer a fully compliant financial service for the cannabis industry, a sector long underserved by traditional banking. Through a unique partnership with an innovative bank, our experienced team has bridged the gap that others failed to address, providing secure banking and card payment solutions to dispensaries. With our expertise in high-risk industries like gaming and gambling, we’re poised to capture a significant share of the $33-billion cannabis market, unlocking substantial revenue potential as the exclusive compliant provider.
